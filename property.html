Architectural Philosophy

Defined by a rigorous adherence to Scandinavian minimalism, The Obsidian Pavilion sits harmoniously within its rocky coastal context. The structure utilizes charred cedar cladding and expansive glass volumes to create a residence that is both a fortress of solitude and an observation deck for the surrounding Baltic Sea.

The interior spaces are curated around a central monolithic fireplace, with seamless transitions between indoor living areas and cantilevered terraces. Every material—from the poured concrete floors to the custom oak cabinetry—has been selected for its longevity and tactile quality.
